      CNY Take note when selected

      • If Yuan(CNY) is selected, Alipay(支付宝) and Union Pay(银联) are the only available transaction methods.
      • However, only debit/credit cards linked to Alipay and Union Pay will be accepted.
      • If you wish to select foreign currency, select Won(KRW).

      KRW Take note when selected

      • If you wish to perform transactions with a foreign currency other than Yuan(CNY), select Won(KRW).
      • The fare shown in the reservation(step) is the amount of Yuan(CNY) converted into Won(KRW).
      • The final transaction amount is equivalent to the amount reconverted from Korean currency to the currency applied to your bank statement.
      • In this case, you may be subject to additional foreign region transaction fees and currency differences.

      Available coupons

      Unaccompanied minors cannot book online separately.

      • Coupons can only be used online, and can be downloaded or entered manually.
      • Discounts can only be applied to additional services, such as flight fares, paid seats, and baggage. Coupons cannot be applied to fuel surcharges and taxes.
      • Coupons may only be applied once and only 1 coupon may be applied to either flight ticket or additional services.
      • Coupons may not be used after valid expiration dates or if the departure and arrival city do not match the coupon.
      • Discount coupons cannot be applied for codeshare or other flights.

      • Coupon discount events may change without notice.
